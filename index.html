Happy Birthday Meri Permanent Duo... ❤️🎂

Pata hai... kabhi kabhi sochta hu ki sirf 2 mahine hi hue hain hame mile? 😂 Sach bolu to lagta hi nahi. Ye 2 mahine itne jaldi nikal gaye ki pata hi nahi chala. Aur itne kam time me aap meri life ka itna important part ban jayengi, ye maine kabhi socha bhi nahi tha.

Aap mere liye sirf ek ingame duo nahi hain. Aap meri bestfriend hain... meri favorite person hain. Aapke saath game khelna, masti karna, random baatein karna aur bas quality time spend karna... ye sab meri daily life ka ek part ban gaya hai. Aur sach bolu, mujhe ye routine bahut pasand hai.

Ek baat jo shayad maine kabhi properly boli hi nahi... Thank You. Thank you hamesha meri side lene ke liye. Chahe situation kuch bhi ho, aap hamesha mere saath khadi rahi hain. Aajkal aise log bahut mushkil se milte hain jo bina kisi wajah ke aapke liye stand lete hain. Isliye mai khud ko lucky maanta hu ki meri life me aap hain.

Aur haan... hamare beech fights bhi hui hain. Kabhi kabhi pura din baat bhi nahi hoti thi. Upar se mai dikhata tha ki mujhe koi farq nahi padta. 😂 Lekin andar se sach kuch aur hi hota tha. Aapke bina kisi cheez me mann hi nahi lagta tha. Game kholta tha to lagta tha kuch missing hai. Bas wait rehta tha ki kab fir se normal baat hogi. Shayad isi ko emotional attachment bolte hain.

Mujhe nahi pata future me meri life me kitne naye log aayenge. Shayad bahut saare. Lekin ek baat mai pure confidence ke saath bol sakta hu... aapki jagah koi nahi le sakta. Aap hamesha meri favorite person rahengi. Aur meri permanent duo bhi. Aapke baad mai kisi aur ko duo banane ka soch bhi nahi sakta, kyunki kuch log replace ho jaate hain... par aap unme se bilkul nahi hain. :)

Bas ek chhoti si wish hai... agar aapko kabhi koi problem na ho, to mai chahta hu ki ham hamesha aise hi rahe. Life me chahe kitne bhi naye log aa jayein, kitni bhi fights ho jayein ya kitna bhi busy ho jayein... bas hamari friendship kabhi khatam na ho.

Aur last me bas itna hi...

Thank You meri life ka itna important part banne ke liye. Thank You meri har bakchodi tolerate karne ke liye. Aur Thank You meri Permanent Duo banne ke liye. ❤️

Happy Birthday once again! Aur haan... apne is pagal duo ko kabhi mat bhulna wrna aapki pitti pitti kar dunga. 🫂❤️
